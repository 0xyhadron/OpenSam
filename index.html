To use this synthesizer, enter words in the input box. When you press the keys, it will iterate through the existing words and audibly speak them. On desktop, you can play the keys that correspond to the user interface. You can modify the variables for "mouth" and "throat" to produce different sounds.

Currently, it just functions as a music player. An interesting thing to note is that the tapes visually change size based on the progress of the song being played. Feel free to experiment and discover more about its functionality.
